About Sights Itinerary

Italy is home to most of the world's art, architecture, fashion, and more. Some of the most famous landmarks are the collosseum, trevi fountain, and the leaning tower of pisa. It is located in south-central Europe, near the Mediterranean Sea. There are approximately 59 million people living in Italy with the population growth rate surprisingly decreasing. Italy acquires almost 40 million visitors annually, making it the fourth most visited country in the world! The country is 116,348 square miles with Rome as the capital and other major cities being Venice, Milan,and Florence. The three main languages spoken are Italian (97.41%), English (13.74%), and French (8.46%). To put in perspective of the size of Italy, it's slightly bigger than the state of Arizona.
