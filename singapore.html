📍Singapore

About Sights Itinerary

Singapore is an island and city-state located in Southeast Asia. It is most famous for the Changi Airport (housing the world's largest and tallest indoor waterfall), Marina Bay Sands Singapore (featured in the movie "Crazy Rich Asians"), and the abundance of greenery surronding the city. Malaysia and Indonesia are the primary countries that border Singapore. The country's population is 5.9 million; the numbers were declining at one point but Singapore is seeing an increase in the future. Its area covers only 281.3 square miles, about half the size of Rhode Island, making it the smallest country by surface area in Southeast Asia. Singapore is surronded by 64 offshore islands (a popular destination within the country for travelers).


The climate of Singapore is most often described as tropical, with high humidity, rainfall, and uniform hot temperatures. Over the year, the average temperature is around 80°F, with the high being 95°F and lows at 75°F (even in the winter). The lowest ever recorded temperature in Singapore was 66°F in 1934. Singapore's climate is unique because of its position, located just 85 miles north of the Equator (one of the closest cities/countries to it).
